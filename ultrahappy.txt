Happiest days on record?

Happiness is not perceiving anything is missing.

You can induce this by getting your intended things done earlier in the day (therefore more happy existence).
  
Some candidate features:
Highest effect size:
- Making a few hours of intense intellectual effort on something necessary or that I care about
- Waking up before dawn
- Intense physical effort or training (/especially/ flow state climbs or runs)
- Waking up early
- Sleeping early
- Hot tea: green tea, others
- *long walks* with friends
- Unstructured time with friends
- Eating "light" foods, nothing digestively intense

Lower effect size / less easy to activate:
- Watching good (should be comedy) TV shows with other people
